FEDERICA ZIACO — 2025
[ 01 — BIO ]

Some people might argue that experience is everything — I proved that passion and persistence can be just as powerful.

My journey into the creative field began in high school, where I specialised in set design. During that time I developed skills in architectural and design software, scriptwriting, storytelling, and illustration.

Spending three of those years at home during COVID-19 led me to reflect deeply on my future in the arts. That period of uncertainty opened the door to a new path — I enrolled at Suor Orsola Benincasa University, where I'm currently studying Linguistics for New Technologies, a multidisciplinary programme bridging language, technology, and communication.

Driven by curiosity and adaptability, I've built a foundation that blends creativity with technology — constantly exploring new ways to turn ideas into impactful experiences. During university I took Web Development courses and worked a summer internship as a Web Designer in London.

My hobbies are a major source of inspiration in my work at the Apple Developer Academy. With a deep passion for music and video games, I've long dreamed of becoming a game designer — and having the chance to actually create games there has been one of the most rewarding experiences so far.
