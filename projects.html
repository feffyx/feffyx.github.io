Molly's Canvas

UX / UI Designer, Game Artist

A psychological thriller game that tells the story of Molly,a girl struggling with anorexia. Mental health topics, especially eating disorders, are often misunderstood or stigmatized. Traditional awareness methods fail to create empathy and understanding.Through interactive storytelling and emotional visualization, players experience Molly's journey firsthand, building empathy and understanding of mental health struggles. The game has been praised by mental health professionals who helped us during the process, and the feedback we received gave was what we hoped for: people understood and empathized with the topic.
