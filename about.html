About Me

Some people might argue that experience is everything, but I proved that passion and persistence can be just as powerful.
My journey into the creative field began in high school, where I specialized in set design. During that time, I developed skills in architectural and design software, scriptwriting, storytelling, and illustration.
However, spending three of those years at home due to the COVID-19 pandemic led me to reflect deeply on my future in the arts.
This period of uncertainty opened the door to a new path. I enrolled at Suor Orsola Benincasa University, where I’m currently studying Linguistics for New Technologies—a multidisciplinary program that bridges language, technology, and communication.
My studies include informatics, advertising, AI fundamentals, and legal aspects of digital innovation. Driven by curiosity and adaptability, I’ve built a foundation that blends creativity with technology constantly exploring new ways to turn ideas into impactful experiences, that's why during my time at university I have also taken Web Development courses and worked a summer internship as a Web Designer in London.
My hobbies are a major source of inspiration in the work I do at the Apple Developer Academy. With a deep passion for music and video games, I’ve long dreamed of becoming a game designer. Having the opportunity to actually create games at the Academy has been one of the most rewarding experiences so far.
It allowed me to explore and grow in areas I’m truly passionate about—such as audio design, illustration, and scriptwriting.
